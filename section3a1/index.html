A Weird Chicken Soup Recipe For You and Me

Gather around for a culinary embrace with this classic chicken noodle soup, a timeless dish that warms the heart as much as it satisfies the palate. This soup isn't just a meal; it's a soothing embrace in a bowl, a reminder of Art of the Web (Fall 2023), love, and the simple joys of a well-crafted dish. Whether you're seeking solace on a chilly evening or a nourishing repast to share with loved ones, this chicken noodle soup stands as a testament to the timeless beauty of simple, heartfelt cooking.



Ingredients: Olive oil, Olive oil, Onion, Carrot, Celery, Garlic, Chicken broth, Bay leaves, Salt, Chicken, Egg noodles


Chefs: Play sound of a stove turning on.


Rule 1: Olive Oil

When you hear the sound of the stove turning on, shout: “The stove's on! Olive oil enters the pot.” Enter the pot, then say “It's time to chop the onion.”


Rule 2: Onion

You will be chopped. Wait until the chefs approach you. They will mimic chopping motions. After they're done, enter the pot and say, “Chopped onion has entered the pot. It’s time to chop the carrot.”


Rule 3: Carrot

You will be chopped. Wait until the chefs approach you. They will mimic chopping motions. After they're done, enter the pot and say, “Chopped carrot has entered the pot. It’s time to chop the celery.”


Rule 4: Celery

You will be chopped. Wait until the chefs approach you. They will mimic chopping motions. After theyre done, enter the pot and say, “Chopped celery has entered the pot. It’s time to mince the garlic.”


Rule 5: Garlic

You will be minced. Wait until the chefs approach you. They will mimic chopping motions. After they're done, enter the pot and say, “Minced garlic has entered the pot. It’s time to pour the chicken broth.”


Rule 6: Chicken Broth

When you hear someone say, “It's time to pour the chicken broth,” you will be poured into the pot. Make a swimming motion to the pot. Stay in the pot. Then, say, “It’s time to add the bay leaves.”


Rule 7: Bay Leaves

When you hear someone say, “It's time to add the bay leaves,” you will be added to the soup to make the dish more flavorful. Make a swaying motion into the pot.Then, say, “Let’s add salt!”


Rule 8: Salt

When you hear someone say, “Let's add salt,” you will be added into the dish to bring out more flavor. Make jazz hands into the pot. Then, say, “Boiling time!”


Chefs: Play boiling sounds


Rule 9: Chicken

When you hear the sounds of boiling soup, you will be added into the pot. With your arms folded as wings, making “Bawk! Bawk!” noises, and walk into the pot. Then, say, “Egg noodles!”


Rule 9: Egg Noodles

When you hear someone say, “Egg Noodles!”, you will be added into the dish. Then, wiggle your arms while walking into the pot. Then, say, “This soup is almost ready!!”


Chefs: Mix the pot. Play a ding! Say, "Bon Appetit!"
